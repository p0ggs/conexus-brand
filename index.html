Welcome to the Conexus Wealth Brand Guidelines

This website contains everything you need to use the Conexus Wealth brand identity correctly. Whether you're creating marketing materials, presentations, or digital content, these guidelines will help you maintain a consistent and professional brand presence.

What is this site for?

This guide website is your one-stop resource for all Conexus Wealth brand assets and specifications. It provides:

  • Logo files in different formats and colour variations for various uses
  • Brand colours with exact colour codes (HEX, RGB, CMYK) to can copy and use
  • Typography guidelines detailing which fonts to use, with download links
  • Textures & backgrounds (coming soon) to enhance brand styling
  • Application examples (coming soon) showing examples of how the brand should be applied

Logo

The logo's strong, sans-serif typography for "CONEXUS" is balanced by the elegant serif font of "Wealth" - creating a visually dynamic and professional look.
The extension of the "X" links the two words together, and the shapes within its negative space offer an opportunity for foil-print detailing as well as a flexible ident shape that can be used autonomously across any branded material.

PNG files are ideal for web use, presentations, documents, and social media. Use PNG when you need a fixed-size raster image that works across all platforms.

SVG files are vector graphics that scale without quality loss. Use SVG for web development, print design, and when working in design software like Illustrator or InDesign where you may need to edit or customise the logo.

Logo Proportions & Spacing

The logo has been aligned with a baseline grid, ensuring a minimum spacing of 3rem (48px at 16px base) between the logo and surrounding artwork in all directions. This spacing is the minimum requirement, but can be increased as needed.

Identity Shapes

The shapes in the negative "X" spaces can be used flexibly as background elements or as points of interest in branded materials.
